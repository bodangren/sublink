# Product Definition - SubLink

## Initial Concept
SubLink: A local-first mobile utility for subcontractors to manage lien waivers, insurance compliance, and photo-verified tasking in underserved construction niches.

## Vision
SubLink empowers subcontractors to professionalize their operations and get paid faster by digitizing the "analog" proof-of-work and compliance bottlenecks in the construction industry.

## Target Audience
- **Solo Operators:** Electricians, plumbers, and HVAC technicians.
- **Micro-Crews:** Small teams (2-5 people) working on residential or light commercial projects.

## Core Features
1. **Digital Lien Waivers:** Generate, sign, and export standardized lien waivers as PDFs directly from the job site.
2. **Compliance Vault:** Store and track Certificates of Insurance (COI) with automated expiration alerts.
3. **Photo-Verified Tasks:** Link photos to specific contract line items. Photos are watermarked with GPS coordinates and timestamps for indisputable proof of work.
4. **Offline-First Workflow:** Full functionality in basements, rural areas, or high-density steel buildings with zero connectivity. Data syncs once a connection is restored.

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in payment delays for subcontractors.
- Zero "missing paperwork" incidents for active projects.
- User adoption among traditionally analog operators.
