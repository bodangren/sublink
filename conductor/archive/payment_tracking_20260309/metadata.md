# Track: Payment Tracking

**Created:** 2026-03-09
**Status:** Completed
**Priority:** High

## Summary
Implement payment tracking to record payments received on invoices, supporting partial payments, multiple payment methods, and payment history.

## Value
- Directly addresses success metric: "Reduction in payment delays for subcontractors"
- Enables tracking of partial payments (common in construction)
- Provides payment history and audit trail
- Works offline-first (record payments on job site)

## Scope
- Payment database schema and operations
- Payment form component
- Payment list/history component
- Integration with invoice detail page
- Dashboard widget for recent payments
- Payment summary in invoice list
